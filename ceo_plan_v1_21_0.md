## Vision

### 10x Check
The 10x version of AetherMind is a living research partner that runs entirely on your machine. A researcher types one sentence: "Add this concept to my graph and connect it to what I already know about scaling laws." AetherMind creates the node, draws the connections, and surfaces older notes the researcher forgot they wrote. No folder navigation. No manual linking. Knowledge integrates from conversation.

### Platonic Ideal
You speak or type a thought → AI decides whether it's a new node, an addition to an existing node, or a connection. The graph grows from your thinking automatically. Every edge has an explanation on demand. The whole thing runs locally — no account, no cloud, no bill.
